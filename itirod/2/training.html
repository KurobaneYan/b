ISACA's four day courses

ISACA's four-day courses provide professional development for those in the audit, assurance and security fields, and ensure that organizations have employees who are armed with the right information and insights to make better informed IT and business decisions.

The opportunities to gain knowledge, meet an industry expert, and expand a network of professional contacts are unparalleled. Attendees return to the office prepared to immediately apply what they learned and with full documentation that can be shared with colleagues.

Training programs

Network Security Auditing
- The Network Security Auditing Course teaches students how to evaluate security risks from the perspective of an attacker allowing students to understand vulnerabilities, and what to audit and why. Students will acquire a baseline knowledge of networking devices and architecture, hackers and their techniques, and security auditing tools and templates.

Taking the Next Step: Advancing Your IT Auditing Skills
- Learn the technical and necessary details to be able to effectively address every aspect of IT Auditing in your organization. Deep dive into how to audit Networks, various Operating Systems, Database environments, Resilience and Cloud Computing.
